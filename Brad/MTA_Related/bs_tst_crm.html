The Chandra Radiation Model (CRM) from Sverdrup/MSFC is propagated in this directory, and graphical and tabular data products are updated every 5 minutes by cron jobs, to track current and future orbital proton flux and fluence. This is a part of TST related collections of scripts.

Please read /data/mta4/proj/rac/ops/README and /data/mta4/proj/rac/ops/CRM/README file for more details.
