Limit Table

Last Updated: Sep 24, 2014

Description

This set of scripts estimates yellow and red limits of all msids from the past data. The limits are derived from average and standard deviations of the last 6 monhts data. The scripts also give the trend plots of these limits, along trend plots and envelope plots, from the beginning of the Chandra mission.
